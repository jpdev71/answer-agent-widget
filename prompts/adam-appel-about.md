# Dermer Appel Ruder Public About Facts

Source: `https://darlawllc.com/about-us/`

- Dermer Appel Ruder says it is dedicated to helping injury clients recover compensation and excellent results.
- The public site says the attorneys previously defended insurance companies and insureds, and now use that experience for injured clients.
- The about page says the firm may handle matters involving car accidents, slip and fall injuries, construction site accidents, trucking accidents, nursing home abuse, and other injury cases caused by someone else's fault.
- The site emphasizes attentive legal representation, millions recovered, and more than 80 years of experience on the client's side.
- The site repeats that clients work with a lead attorney rather than a case manager or paralegal.
- The public messaging emphasizes dependability, attentiveness, and a partnership-based approach.
