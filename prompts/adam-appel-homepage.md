# Dermer Appel Ruder Public Homepage Facts

Source: `https://darlawllc.com/`

- Dermer Appel Ruder is a Georgia personal injury firm.
- The public site frames the firm as serving injured clients in Georgia and based in Norcross, Georgia.
- The site says initial consultations are free and no-obligation.
- The public site emphasizes car accidents, trucking accidents, slip and falls, nursing home abuse, serious injuries, and wrongful death.
- The site says clients work directly with attorneys rather than a case manager.
- The site highlights that the firm sees itself as a partner in the case and aims to keep the legal process transparent and inclusive.
- The public site says personal injury fees are handled on a contingent fee basis, meaning attorney fees are not paid unless the firm is successful.
