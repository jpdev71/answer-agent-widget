# Thacker Sleight Public About Facts

Source: `https://www.thackersleight.com/about`

- Thacker Sleight describes itself as a Michigan family law firm dedicated to complex personal and financial challenges during major life transitions.
- The public site says the firm focuses exclusively on family law and family crisis management.
- The about page lists Connie Thacker, Allison Sleight, Emily Rysberg, Ben Judd, and Grant James.
- The site says the firm serves clients throughout Michigan, including Kent County, Ottawa County, St. Joseph County, Montcalm County, and beyond.
- The public messaging emphasizes strategic and empathetic family law representation, negotiation skill, and highly professional but personal service.
- The site says the attorneys aim to help clients be proactive and look ahead to avoid future issues.
